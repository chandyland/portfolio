STUDENT SHORT FILM COMPETITION


2013 CMU International Film Festival and Department of Media Arts at Robert Morris Univeristy Co-Present Student Film Competition


about the competition


This year's Student Short Film Competition, open to all undergraduate and graduate students at accredited institutions, aims to gather perspectives from a group of diverse student filmmakers and animators from across the world. The film should address the issue of Media, including but not limited to the societal effects of rapid globalized media development, such as violence, (in)justice, identity transformation, voyeurism, obsession, networking, and alienation.

submission guidelines


Requirements


Subtitles

SUBMISSIONS MUST INCLUDE ENGLISH SUBTITLES. Please contact us if this presents a problem.


Short Film Length

The maximum runtime of each submission is 15 minutes.


Number of Submissions

A maximum of two short films may be submitted per director.


Submission Fee

$20. The fee remains twenty dollars whether you are submitting one or two films. Please contact us if this presents a problem.


Format

1. MP4 format 2. Use H.264 codec 3. Code Rate: 5M per second 4. Video size: 720P (1280x720)


Year

Films that were produced before 2011 are not eligible for review.


Deadline

All entries must be submitted by March 18, 2013. Submissions received after that date will not be eligible for review.


Screening

Selected submissions will be played in the Student Short Film Competition Night and will also be posted on the 2013 CMU Faces Official YouTube Channel.
