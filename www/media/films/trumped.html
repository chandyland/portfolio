What happens when money is more powerful than people? In 2006, Donald Trump purchased an estate on the northeast coast of Scotland, planning to build a complex of golf courses, luxury flats, and a grand hotel. The local council rejected his proposal on account of its threat the area's precious, endangered ecosystem. However, the Scottish government overturned the decision and gave a green light to the development of the complex. This documentary explores the controversy that arose in the wake of this devastating decision and the 21st Century David and Goliath battle that ensued. You've Been Trumped is an entertaining, inspiring, and occasionally disturbing parable of our times and values. @youvebeentrumped

About the Director



Filmmaker and journalist Anthony Baxter has worked for many years in England and Scotland making television documentaries for the BBC and Channel 4 among other networks. His work has taken him throughout Central Asia and Africa and has included a stint co-producing camera and editing training courses for young filmmakers in Afghanistan. Now based in Scotland, Anthony is the founder of Montrose Pictures. You've Been Trumped is his first feature-length documentary.
