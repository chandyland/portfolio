The age of social networking and reality TV brings new meaning to the term "intimacy." InContact depicts the interconnected lives of Gen and Christina, two young women "signed-in" to a world of simultaneous performance and spectatorship. Gen and Christina connect through the online social platform "InContact", which broadcasts its users' daily experiences over a series of live video feeds. Director Ann Oren invites the audience into this voyeuristic world as we, like the characters, sit back and watch as tensions rise and drama unfolds through the eyes of social media. @incontact

About the Director



Ann Oren is a Tel-Aviv born video installation artist who currently resides in New York. She received a BFA in Film & Video and an MFA in Fine Arts from the School of Visual Arts, NY. She also attended the Skowhegan School of Art. She has debuted a number of screenings and exhibitions in New York, Los Angeles, and abroad. InContact is her first feature film.
