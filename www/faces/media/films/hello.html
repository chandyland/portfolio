Gabriela and Gabriel are in a dull and passionless marriage. Using online chatrooms, they both find excitement in sharing personal and intimate details about their lives with a complete stranger. The funny thing is that Gabriela and Gabriel are unknowingly talking to each other. Throughout the movie, their teenage son, Vladimir, provides his own comical commentary about his personal struggles and his thoughts about his parents. This film is a refreshing take on the humorously unpredictable nature of love, relationships, and life.

About the Director



Born in 1970 in Bucharest, Alexandru Maftei is a director, scriptwriter and photographer whose student film Night within a Day was presented at many festivals. Since his graduation, Maftei has worked as artistic director at top advertising agencies in Romania, garnering many international awards. While making commercials, he undertook his first feature film project, Hello! How Are You?, casting his wife, Dana Voicu, in the main role. He has stated that he made this romantic comedy because he wants people to feel good for a change when they watch a Romanian movie. Maftei is currently planning to make a historical drama about Stephen the Great of Moldavia. At the Karlovy Vary festival in 2011, Alexandru Maftei landed on Variety's list of ten Euro directors to watch. @alexandru-maftei
