Who We Are

Background

In March 2011, Jacob Buckman and Tony Pezzullo, then high school juniors, received their final SAT scores. When they heard of their friends' struggles with the testing process, they decided they wanted to help. On that day, Stonehill SAT Tutoring was born. Stonehill wants to give back to the community from which it came. Our goal is to help local students succeed on standardized testing by tutoring them at unprecedentedly low rates, the company remains true to that mission, servicing students throughout Monmouth, Mercer, Middlesex, and Ocean counties. We will not be satisfied until every student in central New Jersey has the scores to get them into the college of their dreams!
